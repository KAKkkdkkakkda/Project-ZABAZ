📖 WHAT DO THESE MEAN?
Steady state (ȳ) — Constant equilibrium without shocks. The simplest benchmark.
Efficient output (y*) — Flexible prices + zero markup. The first-best (Pareto optimum).
Natural output (yⁿ) — Flexible prices but positive steady-state markup (monopolistic competition). What central banks call "potential".
HP trend — Statistical Hodrick-Prescott filter (λ=1600). Purely data-driven, no economic structure.
